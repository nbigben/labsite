Yuan-Chung Cheng’s Research Group

鄭原忠老師理論物理化學研究室

 
 

We are a theoretical physical chemistry group that studies quantum dynamical phenomena in molecular systems relevant to advanced materials and energy applications. We aim to develop new theoretical tools as well as molecular modeling techniques to investigate the dynamics of light harvesting, photon-induced chemical dynamics, ultrafast nonlinear spectroscopy, and  properties of optoelectronic nano-materials.
